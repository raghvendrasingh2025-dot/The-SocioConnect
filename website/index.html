10 · FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How much does a professional website cost?

A bespoke website starts from $1,500 for a standard showcase site, $3,500 for complex portals with admissions/ERP connections, and up to $6,000+ for enterprise-level custom builds. Every estimate is custom-tailored to your features and integrations.

How long does it take to develop a site?

Typically, a standard showcase website takes 3 to 4 weeks, while an ERP-enabled school site or detailed custom e-commerce portal takes 6 to 8 weeks. We divide the process into transparent milestones with active client sign-offs.

Are the websites optimized for SEO?

Absolutely. Every line of code is structured semantically for search engines. We set up custom meta tags, clean URL structures, sitemaps, semantic schemas, and optimize Core Web Vitals to guarantee high page speeds and better search engine positioning.

Do you offer maintenance after launch?

Yes. Every site includes 3 months of free post-launch support for bug fixes and small tweaks. Afterwards, we offer monthly maintenance packages starting from $99/month, covering security updates, hosting management, backups, and regular SEO performance checkups.
